# Claim-Evidence Registry

本文件用于防止把尚未完成的实验写成论文结论。每条主张必须绑定证据来源。

| Claim ID | Manuscript claim | Required evidence | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | Existing MGT detectors degrade under unseen domains and generators. | Cross-domain and cross-generator test results on MAGE/SemEval/RAID; prior literature citations. | `needs evidence` | Literature supports the concern, but local results are required. |
| C2 | Calibration reduces overconfident errors under distribution shift. | ECE, reliability diagrams, Brier score before/after calibration. | `needs evidence` | Must avoid saying "improves accuracy" unless accuracy improves. |
| C3 | Selective prediction can maintain lower risk by abstaining on uncertain cases. | Coverage-risk curves; selective risk at fixed coverage. | `needs evidence` | Need define deployment policy and coverage threshold. |
| C4 | Adversarial perturbations expose different failure modes from ordinary OOD transfer. | Perturbation-specific error analysis on RAID or generated attacks. | `needs evidence` | Need ensure perturbation types are licensed and reproducible. |
| C5 | The proposed framework is useful for practical content-risk workflows. | Scenario analysis plus quantitative selective prediction results. | `needs evidence` | Keep bounded; do not imply legal proof of authorship. |
| C6 | The method is reproducible and reusable. | Code, environment, data instructions, random seeds, split manifests. | `needs evidence` | Must be true before submission. |

## Current permissible wording

Use:

- "This study evaluates..."
- "The proposed protocol is designed to test..."
- "We will report..."
- "The current draft keeps unresolved claims as explicit evidence gaps."

Do not use until experiments are complete:

- "Our method outperforms..."
- "The proposed method significantly improves..."
- "We demonstrate..."
- "The detector is robust..."
- "The first..."
